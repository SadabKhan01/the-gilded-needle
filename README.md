# The Gilded Needle — A Tailor's Tale

A cozy pixel-art tailor RPG for the browser, in the spirit of **Stardew Valley** and **Chef RPG**.

You are **Marielle Thimm**, a young tailor from the mountain village of Hartsfell. Armed with her
late grandmother's gilded needle and a one-way train ticket, she arrives in the grand city of
**Auberlin** to take over a dusty little shop on Spindle Square — and to mend a sixty-year-old
injustice, one stitch at a time.

## How to play

Open `index.html` in a browser (or serve the folder with any static server):

```sh
cd the-gilded-needle
python3 -m http.server 8080
# then visit http://localhost:8080
```

## The loop

1. **Gather** — venture into four increasingly dangerous zones (the Weftworks, the Madder Docks,
   the Larkspur Silk Garden, the Underspool) to collect 16 kinds of fabric. Mind the lintmoths,
   dye-rats, garden hounds, and the Warden Loom.
2. **Sew** — craft 18 garments at your sewing table with "The Seam," a stitch-timing minigame.
   Better stitches, better stars, better prices.
3. **Sell** — customers visit your shop from 8:00 to 18:00. Serve the poor quarter first, then
   the middle class, the nobility, and at last the royal court.
4. **Rise** — reputation unlocks new districts, fabrics, and recipes — all the way to the
   coronation gown.

## Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| WASD / Arrows | Move |
| E | Talk / Gather (hold) / Use |
| Space | Scissor swipe / Stitch |
| I | Inventory |
| Esc | Menu / Back |

Progress saves when you sleep (and from the pause menu).
